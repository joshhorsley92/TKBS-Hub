### Conversion drivers

- **30-Day Lead Guarantee** (primary): *"If your system isn't generating leads
  within 30 days, we rebuild the underperforming piece at no additional cost."*
- **No contracts:** month-to-month. *"You stay because it's working, not because
  you're locked in."* Expected first leads in 2–4 weeks.

## CRM engagement / deliverable catalog

Inside the CRM an **"engagement"** is the deal/contract unit — a set of priced
service packages tied to a client, each line item billed as **one-time (setup)**
or **monthly (MRR)**. Package slugs: Ad Copy, Ad Images, Ad Hosting, Ad A/B
Testing, Landing Page, Full Website Redesign, Email Copy, Email Reviews, Email
Media, Email Automation, Other.

Engagement sources: Warm Referral, Warm Outreach, Cold Outreach, TKBS Marketing
Material, Social Media, Booked Call with TKBS.
